Our Science

Satellites offer much more than awesome pictures. They sense the Earth with visible, infrared, microwave, and hyperspectral signals. They measure cloudiness, wind speed, and air pollution. They monitor storms, floods and droughts. They quantify rainfall and snowpacks. They detect changes in soil moisture and ground water. They assess the health and productivity of commodity crops. And there are decades of such data – a rich gold mine for data science.

At Sky Informatics, based on our expertise in data science and remote sensing, we integrate a wide array of satellite data, develop analytical models, and create products and insights of high social and commercial impact.

We employ rigorous algorithms and procedures for data aggregation, synthesis, homogenization, interpolation, error detection, error correction, and uncertainty quantification.
